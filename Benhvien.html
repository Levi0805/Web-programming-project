INTRODUCE

Address:   The hospital has many gates, patients who come for examination will go to the gate:  

  • No. 16 - 18 Phu Doan, Hoan Kiem, Hanoi

Working time:  Monday to Saturday

  • Morning: 7:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m
  • Afternoon: 13h30 - 16h30

Viet Duc Hospital is one of the 5 central level hospitals, special class of Vietnam. The hospital has a history of over 100 years, a long tradition of reputation, is the cradle of the surgical industry in Vietnam associated with important medical achievements of the country. 

Viet Duc is a leading prestigious address for surgery, conducting medical examination and treatment, and performing basic and intensive functional scanning, testing and screening techniques every day for people. 

The hospital has a strong medical team, many of whom are also teaching staff at Hanoi Medical University or Faculty of Medicine and Pharmacy - Hanoi National University. Among them many are leading experts and experienced doctors in different specialties. 

Important Note

  • The hospital has many examination areas, so for convenience and saving time when going to the doctor, patients should learn about the location of their examination area before going to the doctor.
  • Viet Duc Friendship Hospital is a hospital specializing in surgery, so the schedule of doctors is often flexible and priority is given to emergency cases.
  • Patients should actively prepare some questions related to their condition before going to the doctor to make the medical examination journey more effective.

Examination costs

Patients can choose from one of the following packages:

  1. Package 1:
    • Examination of Professor, Associate Professor, Doctor, Specialist II - Cost 50euro/visit
    • Examination with the Doctor of the Department or Deputy of the Department - Cost 50euro/visit
  2. Package 2:
    • Doctor's Examination, Specialist Doctor I - Cost: 30euro/visit

LOCATION

Other websites have the address of Viet Duc Hospital at 40 Trang Thi, this is the main gate but usually only for paperwork and administrative work. Patients who come to the clinic will go through the auxiliary gates on Phu Doan Street. 

There are 3 gates on Phu Doan Street and 1 main gate on Trang Thi Street. Patients should go to the right gate as directed to go to the clinic easily and quickly.
